NEW APPLICATION FOR RIDE SHARRING
Smart transportation is all about better usage of our already-existing infrastructure and resources rather than the addition of new ones. Better use of existing resources will create affordable transportation without adding more roads or vehicles.

La'Zooz real-time ridesharing enables people with private cars to share their drive with others traveling in the same direction, not for commercial profit but rather for sharing ride costs and the creating collective benefit by reducing the amount of traffic on the roads.
Traffic

To free transportation from its sickness, we must increase occupancy within the existing infrastructure. Ironically, our current road space and transport expenses are used to move mostly empty seats from one place to another. It is a well-hidden fact that thousands of empty seats haven’t experienced any tush for years.

Resources

The zooz mining protocol creates a stable internal economy based on weighted share of value and fair distribution of community responsibility. We believe this initiative can generate a sustainable and valuable ecosystem.

Collaboration

Collaboration in which anyone can freely contribute. The community collectively decides on the reward in zooz for each contribution via sophisticated protocols. The ‘weight’ of each member’s input is dynamically valued by the community itself. Many people around the world have already taken an active part in this operation, and the community is growing every day.​

About Us

La`Zooz

A Decentralized Transportation Platform owned by the community enabling unused riding space space to be transformed into a variety of smart transportation solutions. By using cryptocurrency technology La`Zooz has developed a “Fair Share” rewarding mechanism for its developers, users and backers.

RideSharing vision

La`Zooz platform will synchronize empty seats with transportation needs in real-time, and match like minded people to create a great ride-sharing experience with a “Fair fare”.

Download Zooz-mining app

The purpose of this mining app is to obtain the critical mass of movment (Users X Distance) needed for the collaborative transportation web to become operational, as well as to create and fairly distribute Zooz tokens among the community. There are two basic ways to increase movement in the system:Send your movement data (i.e. running the app and driving >20kmh).Get others to send their movement data. Both actions are being rewarded with Zooz tokens through La’Zooz mining application. In this way, early adopters of La’Zooz network are gaining value and thus being incentivised to take part in the establishment of the network before its technological benefit takes effect and any transport service is available. Once the critical mass of movement is reached in a certain geographical region, many drivers and riders are out there ready to share rides, and ride-sharing and other smart transportation services will become enabled and viable. The rate of distribution of Zooz tokens via movement-based mining is set to encourage the growth of local La`Zooz communities. Thus, it is proportional not only to the distance you are driving, but also to the growth of the community in your area. which incentivises users to premote the establishment of La`Zooz network in their own region.
